# Ontiver store metadata

**Name:** Ontiver

**Subtitle / short description:** Verify once. Share with control.

**Category:** Utilities / Productivity
**Audience:** Adults aged 18 and over

Ontiver is a secure identity wallet for managing verified credentials, reviewing who has access to identity data, and sharing consent-controlled proofs. Supported identity verification is performed by SmileID. Users can sign in with email, Google, or Apple on iPhone, protect the app with device security, review sessions, export their information, and request permanent account deletion.

Ontiver does not sell personal information. Availability of credential types and verification services depends on jurisdiction and rollout status.
